3.

Goal is to use "arduino style" stacking headers, which are .1" spaced sockets with long leads so they can stack on top of each other.

Turns out this is made by samtec, (product sheet)

we don't require stacking moitherboard side so we use short lead versions, otherwise lead length must be set for a stacking height of at least 15 mm (as of 3/14 per this note)

we have 2 5-pin headers, 1 2x3 header, and 1 2 pin header,

longest header is 14.5mm lead length, with a 1-2mm board this give us ~14 mm of clearance (14.5mm lead minus 1-2mm board thickness + 2-5mm stacking offset), which should be enough to clear the molex 16 min connectors and electrolytics

(Won't affect layout, but check if 10mm header gives adequate clearance!)

LFCSP48 and TQFP-48 (no thermal pad).

For hand soldering we go with TQFP, (package outline)  (footprint #1) (QFP package recomendations )




in mm:
Length of Pad = (A-B)/2 =  0.85
Width of pad = 0.32
Centers offset = 7.7/2 + Length_of_pad/2 = 4.275mm
pad center to pad center = 0.5mm

Recomendation is to add 0.5mm to toe and heel of pad, giving total legnth of 1.8mm and width of 0.3-0.32, 0.3 gives 7 mil spacing between pads. can shrink pad to .28 probably to fit 8 mil spacing, but fusion PCB has 6mil clearance for outside layers so that shouldn't be an issue.
